About

If 60% of Americans vote on Tuesday it will be considered a great turnout. That means 40% of our nation has some excuse for avoiding the polls. Cultivated Wit and the Ford Foundation have teamed together to try and get those excuse makers out to vote—even if it means shaming them into it.
